Quick daily check on growth signals and content pipeline health.

## Daily Actions

1. Check for new user signups (via Paperclip webhook notifications)
2. Review community monitoring for urgent/time-sensitive opportunities
3. Ensure content calendar is on track (are this week's posts drafted?)
4. Flag any viral or high-engagement content opportunities to the CEO

## Output

Brief daily comment (3-5 bullet points) covering: new signups, community signals worth noting, content pipeline status, any action items.
