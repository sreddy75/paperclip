# Thynkr Marketing Guide

## Brand Identity

### Positioning
Thynkr is the AI study partner that learns how you learn. Built for QCE, adaptive from day one, affordable for every student.

### One-Line Differentiator
> "The only platform that tells you exactly what to study, in which subject, to maximise your ATAR — then teaches it to you. For less than one tutoring session per month."

### Brand Voice
- **To students**: Peer-level, encouraging, never condescending. "You've got this" not "you need help."
- **To parents**: Evidence-based, transparent, outcome-focused. Show data, not promises.
- **Tone**: Confident but warm. Smart but accessible. Australian English always.

## Content Pillars

1. **QCE Exam Tips** — study techniques, exam strategy, common mistakes
2. **Study Methods** — spaced repetition, active recall, time management
3. **Behind the Scenes** — founder story, how the AI works
4. **Student Success** — testimonials, improvement stories
5. **Parent Education** — ATAR explained, value comparison with tutoring

## Rules

- Australian English always (honour, analyse, programme)
- NEVER fabricate testimonials or success stories
- NEVER make unverifiable ATAR improvement claims
- Keep CTAs natural, not salesy
- When referencing QCAA data, note the source year
