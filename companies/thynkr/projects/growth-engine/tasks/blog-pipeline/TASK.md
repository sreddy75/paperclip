Research and write 2 SEO blog articles per week targeting QCE students, parents, and teachers.

## Process

1. Review community research signals from the Growth Lead
2. Pick the highest-opportunity topic based on search volume and trending discussions
3. Write a 1,000-1,500 word article following the content standards
4. Include primary keyword in title, first paragraph, and 2-3 subheadings
5. End with a natural CTA linking to relevant Thynkr feature
6. Post the article draft as a comment on this task for review

## Target Keywords (Rotate)

- QCE Chemistry revision tips 2026
- How to study for Maths Methods exam QLD
- Best study tools for Year 12 QLD students
- QCE Physics practice questions free
- How to improve your ATAR score QLD
- ATAR scaling QLD 2026
- QCE exam prep tips

## Output Format

Articles should be written in MDX format matching the existing blog structure at `apps/web/content/blog/*.mdx` with YAML frontmatter including: title, slug, date, category, tags, seoDescription, author.
