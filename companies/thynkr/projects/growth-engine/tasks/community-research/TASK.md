Monitor QCE student communities daily for trends, pain points, and content opportunities.

## Platforms to Monitor

- **Reddit**: r/QCEstudents, r/AustralianTeachers, r/ATAR
- **Discord**: Queensland Study Hub
- **Facebook**: QCE parent groups, Year 12 study groups
- **X/Twitter**: #QCE2026, #ATAR, #Year12QLD
- **Whirlpool**: Education forums

## What to Look For

- Trending topics or questions students are asking
- Pain points with studying, exams, or ATAR
- Misconceptions about ATAR scaling or subject selection
- Competitor mentions (Atomi, Edrolo, Education Perfect)
- Content opportunities (questions that could become blog posts)

## Output

Post a daily summary comment on this task with:
- Top 3-5 signals found
- Platform and community source
- Engagement numbers (upvotes, comments, likes)
- Recommended content angle if applicable
- Urgency assessment (time-sensitive or evergreen)
